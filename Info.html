Why are Snow Leopards Endangered?

  1. They are hunted a lot for their fur and their bones are used in medicine.
  2. They are poached for illegal trade.
  3. Habitat loss is occuring due to human population increase and land expansion.
  4. Climate change affects a lot of ecosystems negatively. Snow Leopards live in cold enviroments and climate change is causing extreme weather events and shrinking the alpine zone where Snow Leopards live.
  5. Snow Leopards are experiencing human-wildlife conflict because there is a decrease in their food which is causing them to turn to domesticated livestock. Humans will kill Snow Leopards to save their livestock.
  6. There is a decrease in their prey because humans are overhunting their prey.
